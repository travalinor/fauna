Wildlife watching, done right

Fauna is being built to give wildlife travellers the one thing that doesn't exist yet: a single place to find what's out there, where to go, and whether the experience is genuinely good for the animals involved.

🌍

Global scope

All vertebrate species, anywhere in the world. Not just birds, not just safari.

⭐

Ethics framework

Every experience rated on four criteria, developed with conservation partners.

🔬

Citizen science

Your sightings contribute to conservation monitoring via iNaturalist and GBIF.

🗺

Trip planning

Know when to go, where to look, and who to trust before you book anything.
